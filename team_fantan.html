Meet our Team

We invite you to discover the collective brilliance of our team, the visionaries who passionately shape the landscape of risk excellence. Get to know the individuals behind the expertise, each contributing their unique talents to the dynamic tapestry of REB's success.

Mareledi M. Fantan

Executive Chairman

Mareledi M. Fantan (MMF) is a seasoned Engineer and Project Manager with strong background in Risk Management. With extensive experience gained over 4 decades MMF has tackled challenges at the intersection of technical excellence and risk mitigation. MMF is well-versed in steering projects and managing risks throughout the project lifecycle. He championed a diverse range of projects, most of which was during his time at Debswana where he worked for 29 years and rose through the ranks to the position of Group Manager for Projects & Engineering.

In all the projects that were undertaken over the years of his tenure in the projects management sphere, each project had to undergo a risk identification, and classification culminating in mitigation or avoidance. This would ultimately be translated into a cost of insuring against or engineering out such risk.

  • BSc in Electrical and Electronics Engineering (England, UK)

Thatayaone Sesinyi

COO

Thatayaone Sesinyi (TS) is a distinguished risk professional whose expertise resonates at the nexus of Chemical/ Process Engineering, Enterprise Risk Management, Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) considerations, and Developmental strategies. Building upon this technical acumen, TS pursued an MBA, which cultivated a keen understanding of business dynamics, strategic planning, and financial management.

Having achieved the prestigious COSO Enterprise Risk Management (ERM) Certification, TS demonstrates an unparalleled command over risk management methodologies and practices. This certification validates his ability to identify, assess, and mitigate risks across diverse organizational landscapes, fostering a culture of proactive risk awareness and mitigation

In a rapidly evolving business landscape that places increasing emphasis on sustainability and responsible business practices, Mr. Sesinyi shines as an ESG Specialist and brings strategic perspective to integrating ESG principles into risk management strategies, ensuring organizations navigate risks while maintaining a positive impact on society and the environment.

  • MEng/BEng in Chemical & Mineral Engineering (England, UK), MBA (Wales), ESG Specialist, COSO ERM Certified.

Paul G Ramokgalo

Founder and CEO

Paul G. Ramokgalo (PGR) is a trailblazing risk professional whose visionary leadership and dynamic expertise have reshaped and continues to shape the landscape of risk management in the country. With an illustrious career of over 30 years, PGR has walked the Risk Management value chain end to end, weaving together intricate threads of risk assessment, mitigation, insurance innovation, and claims management

PGR spent 14 years in Debswana where he established the Risk Management Function - instituting proactive risk mitigation strategies, and fostering a culture of risk-awareness that permeates all facets of Debswana’s operations. Beyond traditional risk management, PGR charted unexplored territory by establishing Debswana’s captive insurance company (Botswana’s first captive) to optimize risk financing strategies. Paul continues to facilitate risk discussions through consultancy work and various Board appointments.

  • MSc/BA in Risk Management, (Scotland, UK), CMIRM (UK), AIISA (SA)
